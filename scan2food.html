Overview

Scan2Food is a modern web application designed to revolutionize food ordering in theatres. Customers can order snacks and beverages directly from their seats, while theatre staff and administrators manage orders efficiently. The app leverages WebSockets for live order updates, SSE for smooth data streaming, and multiple micro-apps for theatre, staff, admin, and customer management. Integration with WhatsApp API ensures instant notifications for orders, refunds, and customer support. Scan2Food has quickly gained popularity and carved its own place in the market by enhancing the theatre experience.
